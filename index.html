HAWTREE RESCUE OF NEW YORK




About

Hawtree Rescue (aka the Hawtree First Aid Squad), founded in 1989, is a non-profit, combination (volunteer & career) first aid squad/agency, best know for our innovative patient care solutions, and the outstanding treatment we provide to each and every member of our served communities. Our team is currently comprised of 17 dedicated volunteers and 5 paid employees, serving in all types of medical and administrative roles.

Primarily serving the "Downstate New York" area, we've also served our neighboring communities through mutual aid contracts for over 25 years, as well as provided communications assistance and medical support for local gatherings, community events, and more.

Made with Love.

Hawtree Rescue was founded in 1989, by a group of dedicated community members that saw the need for a volunteer first aid squad in their area at a time that not many else did. Their actions led to many lives being saved, with more to come.
